# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Vaani to be a welcoming, harassment‑free project for everyone, regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Be excellent to each other

- Be respectful, patient, and kind.
- Welcome newcomers and differing viewpoints.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users.

Not acceptable: harassment, insults, discrimination, personal attacks, or sharing others' private information.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — and when representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **divyamohan1993@gmail.com**. Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove content or ban contributors who violate this code.

This is a condensed adaptation of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), v2.1.
